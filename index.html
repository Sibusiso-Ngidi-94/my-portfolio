Reducing checkout drop-off by 42%
UX Research
Problem

Process

02
Achieving 98 Lighthouse performance score
Performance
Problem
Dashboard was sluggish at scale — 4s LCP, layout shifts, and janky chart animations on lower-end devices.
Process
Profiled with Chrome DevTools, virtualised lists, lazy-loaded chart modules, and replaced D3 DOM mutations with canvas.
Result
LCP improved from 4.1s to 0.8s. Lighthouse score went from 61 to 98. Zero layout shift achieved.
